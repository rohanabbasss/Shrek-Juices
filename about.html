About The Bar

Shrek Juices is an establishment that primarily serves prepared juice beverages such as freshly squeezed or extracted fruit juices, juice blends, fruit smoothies (a thick fruit drink, often iced), or other juices such as fresh wheatgrass juice.

Shrek Juice's History

Meaning long, beautiful life, embodies the promise that flows from the beverages it delivers in each and every bottle. Shrek Juice’s story began in 2012 from a shared dream to help people everywhere transform their lives through conscious nutrition. Responding to a lack of clean, nutritious, and convenient beverages available, Shrek Juice’s founders crafted a line of organic juices as a convenient way to live a plant-powered lifestyle. What started as delivering cold-pressed juice on a skateboard around San Diego, has grown to a range of nationally-available, high quality, organic and functional beverages, including our slow brewed kombucha and functional shots. Today, we carry on our founders’ mission to get the best quality organic and non-GMO beverages into as many hands as possible. Through the large-scale use of local, organic fruits and vegetables, and implementation of a breakthrough displacement technology called High Pressure Processing (HPP), Shrek Juices has become the nation’s leading organic juice brand.

Why Choose Us

We re dedicated to health and wellness, and to the power of positivity. These core principles are at the heart of the Clean Juice offering and are part of why we have seen tremendous growth in a very short time.

Fresh Juice

Fruit juice is 100% pure juice made from the flesh of fresh fruit or from whole fruit, depending on the type used. It is not permitted to add sugars, sweeteners, preservatives, flavourings or colourings to fruit juice.
